date: 2021-02-24 11:09:47
description: YouTube Recommended Resolution
---

For the default 16:9 aspect ratio, encode at these resolutions:

- **2160p**: 3840x2160
- **1440p**: 2560x1440
- **1080p**: 1920x1080
- **720p**: 1280x720
- **480p**: 854x480
- **360p**: 640x360
- **240p**: 426x240
